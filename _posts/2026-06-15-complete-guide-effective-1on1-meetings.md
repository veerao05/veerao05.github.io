## Why 1:1s Matter

One-on-one meetings are one of the most underutilised tools in an engineer's career toolkit. When done right, they accelerate growth, surface blockers early, and build the trust needed for high-performing teams.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating them as status updates instead of growth conversations
- Not preparing an agenda beforehand
- Letting them get cancelled repeatedly without rescheduling
- Focusing only on immediate tasks, not long-term development

## Tips for Effective 1:1s

Whether you're the manager or the engineer, come with topics ready. Think about: recent wins, current blockers, career goals, feedback you want to give or receive, and team dynamics.
