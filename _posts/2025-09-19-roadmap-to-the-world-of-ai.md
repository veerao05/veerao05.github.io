## Where to Start

The AI world can feel like a firehose of new models, frameworks, and buzzwords. But there's a structured way to approach it as a software engineer:

1. **Foundations First** — Linear algebra, statistics, and Python
2. **ML Basics** — Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
3. **Deep Learning** — Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, Transformers
4. **LLMs & Generative AI** — Prompt engineering, RAG, fine-tuning
5. **MLOps** — Model deployment, monitoring, and CI/CD for ML

## For Backend Engineers

As a Java/Python backend engineer, your existing skills in API design, distributed systems, and cloud infrastructure are highly valuable in the AI space. Focus on how to integrate AI services into production systems.
